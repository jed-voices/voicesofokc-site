About the show

A podcast rooted in Oklahoma City and built with students.

Voices of OKC is a student-produced podcast from City Center featuring leaders, stories, and ideas shaping Oklahoma City. The goal is simple: thoughtful conversations, real civic access, and meaningful opportunities for students to grow through the work itself.

Watch the Show Sponsor the Show

What makes Voices distinct

It is not just a podcast feed. It is a hands-on production environment where students gain real experience in media, storytelling, and leadership.

It is also a civic platform, rooted in Oklahoma City, where meaningful conversations are given room to breathe.

What Voices of OKC is

Thoughtful conversations with real civic weight.

Voices of OKC highlights leaders, founders, mentors, public servants, and voices of hope across Oklahoma City. The tone is thoughtful, clear, and human. The aim is not hype. It is substance.

Each episode is designed to help people better understand the city, the people shaping it, and the values that help communities move forward.

Why student production matters

Students are not standing on the edges.

Voices of OKC gives students real opportunities to host, co-host, ask questions, run production, learn camera and audio workflows, and gain experience in environments that would normally feel out of reach.

That matters because exposure changes imagination. Real responsibility builds confidence. And meaningful access helps students begin to see themselves differently.

Rooted in Oklahoma City

The show is locally grounded. Its guests, stories, and themes are shaped by the city itself and the people helping lead it forward.

Connected to City Center

Voices of OKC is produced through City Center, where relief, restoration, dignity, and opportunity are already part of the larger mission.

Built for depth

Long-form conversation still matters. Voices of OKC creates space for nuance, reflection, and ideas that deserve more than a clipped soundbite.

Who the show is for

Leaders, listeners, and people who care about the city.

Voices of OKC is for people who want more than noise. It is for listeners who care about leadership, community, mentorship, civic life, and hopeful stories that feel grounded in the real world.

It is also for guests and partners who want to help create a better kind of local media experience—one that is thoughtful, dignified, and invested in the next generation.

Where to start

Watch, listen, or get in touch.

You can start with a featured episode, follow the show on your preferred platform, explore sponsorship, or simply connect to learn more.
