SUPPORT CONVERSATIONS THAT MATTER AND THE STUDENTS HELPING PRODUCE THEM.

VOICES of OKC is a student-produced podcast from City Center featuring leaders, stories, and ideas shaping Oklahoma City. Sponsoring the show helps strengthen meaningful local storytelling while investing in real student growth through production, media, and leadership experience.

What your sponsorship supports

Student production experience in interviewing, camera work, audio, lighting, and storytelling.

Conversations with civic leaders, founders, public servants, mentors, and voices of hope across Oklahoma City.

A platform that reflects the dignity, substance, and future of the city it comes from.

Why sponsor

More than ad placement.

VOICES of OKC is built for more than exposure. It is a platform for thoughtful Oklahoma City conversations and a hands-on pathway for students to gain real media and production experience.

That means sponsorship does two things at once: it helps meaningful stories reach people, and it helps students grow in skill, confidence, and opportunity.

Built through City Center. Produced with students. Rooted in Oklahoma City.

COMMON SPONSOR OPPORTUNITIES

Sponsorship can be shaped around fit, audience, and mission alignment. The best partnerships feel clear, useful, and grounded in the same belief: Oklahoma City is worth listening to closely.

  • Season sponsorship for sustained visibility across a full run of conversations.
  • Episode sponsorship connected to a specific guest, topic, or civic theme.
  • Student production support for equipment, training, editing, and hands-on learning.
  • Special collaborations that help thoughtful local stories reach the right audience.
