Episode feature

From Policy to People: Mayor David Holt on leadership, youth, and Oklahoma City

This conversation brings together Mayor David Holt, student host Jordan Arnett, and Jed Chappell for a thoughtful discussion about civic leadership, long-term vision, Oklahoma City’s growth, and why productive pathways for young people matter.

Guest: Mayor David Holt Student Host: Jordan Arnett Recorded at City Hall
Watch on YouTube Spotify Apple Podcasts

What this episode explores

  • What leadership looks like when difficult decisions affect an entire city
  • How Oklahoma City’s growth has taken vision, patience, and long-term public investment
  • Why young people need structure, support, and real opportunities to thrive
  • How civic awareness and persistence shape a more productive future
  • Why places like City Center matter as models for youth development

Why this episode matters

This episode captures the heart of Voices of OKC: meaningful Oklahoma City conversations with real civic weight, paired with real student opportunity. Jordan Arnett is not just present in the room. He is actively helping lead the conversation.

That blend of leadership access and student production is what makes this platform distinct.

Featured moment

“You can grow up in Oklahoma City and literally be anything you want.”

Mayor Holt’s encouragement to young people was clear: opportunity is here, but it takes work, persistence, and the willingness to stay engaged.

Production note

This episode is a strong example of the student-produced mission behind Voices of OKC. The conversation brought together a civic leader, a student host, and a platform designed to give young people real access and responsibility.
