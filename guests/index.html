KNOW A VOICE OKLAHOMA CITY SHOULD HEAR?

Help us find leaders, builders, artists, advocates, entrepreneurs, mentors, and neighbors whose stories can help Oklahoma City see itself more clearly.

What makes a strong nomination

VOICES of OKC looks for guests with lived experience, local responsibility, practical wisdom, or a story that opens up a fuller view of the city.

  • Civic leaders and public servants
  • Builders, founders, and entrepreneurs
  • Artists, storytellers, and culture-makers
  • Mentors, advocates, and community builders
  • Neighbors carrying a story with depth and dignity
